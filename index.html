About Me

I am Afsana Mim and I am a

I work in a company as a web developer, and at the same time, I teach many students. The teaching profession is my hobby, and web development is my passion. I have 2 years of experience in full-stack development. I plan to be an IT entrepreneur in Bangladesh.

After graduating in 2019, I worked as a Web Developer for Race Online Ltd and Mamun Biriany House. My job's leading roles and responsibilities are designing and developing the front-end (with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap), back-end with JavaScript, PHP, and Database (with MySQL). I have done 6 months of Internship and Training at Pencilbox Training Institute as a 'Full Stack Developer', where I have gained Industrial and practical knowledge. For my final year project, I created a website whose design and functionality were all built from Core PHP using Java, JavaScript, PHP, Ajax, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and MySQL called The Fundraising Management System. During my undergraduate programs, I have done many projects such as the Tic Toe game, Chatting Apps like Messenger, Rain alarm, CV making website, and a university website (for practice). I was the team leader of all projects. These projects helped me enhance my practical skills, which are crucial to working in the real world. I had given numerous presentations and demonstrations about my assignments to department heads and respective teachers, whose feedback was always cheerful and appreciative. I am highly motivated and proactive at learning new skills and experiencing new things.
